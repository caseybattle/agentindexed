About AgentIndexed

AgentIndexed exists because finding the right AI agent got harder, not easier. Thousands of agents launched in the last two years; most directories responded with pay-to-play rankings, auto-scraped junk listings, and zero curation.

We do it differently. Every listing on this index is reviewed by a human before it goes live. Categories describe what a tool actually does. Search results are never influenced by payment — featured placements are clearly badged and confined to featured slots.

Curation policy

  • Every submission is manually checked: working product, real website, accurate description.
  • We reject spam, dead links, misleading descriptions, and thin affiliate pages.
  • Paid placement buys position and visibility — never a listing that wouldn't be accepted for free.
  • Listings are periodically re-checked; dead tools get removed.
